WHY WE BUILT IT

Built to be put down.

Most kids' apps are designed to hold attention. Wonder Notes is designed to launch it — fifteen minutes of spark, then mud, cardboard, string, and big questions out in the real world. The screen's job is to start something, not to be the something.

Questions, answered.

Why can't I save to Photos?

Check Settings → Wonder Notes → Photos and choose "Add Photos Only" or "Full Access".

Why is the Big Question locked?

One real answer per day keeps it special — it unlocks again at 6am. You can override from the lock screen if it really can't wait.

Where is my Wonder Journal stored?

Entirely on your device. Deleting the app deletes your journal, so save treasured pages to Photos or print them.

Does the app need an account?

No — there are no accounts, no ads, and no tracking. See our privacy policy.
