Wonder Notes — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 5 June 2026

Wonder Notes is a family discovery app made for parents. It is designed to collect as little data as possible. There are no accounts, no advertising, no tracking, and no third-party analytics SDKs. This page explains exactly what data the app handles and where it goes.

What stays on your device

Almost everything. The following is stored only on your device and is never uploaded to us:

  • Your children's ages and your session settings (location, materials)
  • Your Wonder Journal — recorded moments, your child's dictated words, and photos
  • Photos you take in the app (saved to your device and, if you choose, to your own photo library)
  • App preferences such as onboarding state and the daily question timer

If you delete the app, this data is deleted with it. We have no copy.

What is sent to our servers, and why

When you generate an activity or an answer, the app sends the information needed to create that content — the location label, selected materials, any optional context you typed, your children's ages, and (for the Answer path) the question you typed — to our secure backend, which forwards it to Anthropic's Claude API to generate the educational content. This is processing, not collection: we do not store this content against any identity, and the app contains no account, name, email, or device identifier to attach it to. Anthropic processes API inputs under its commercial terms and does not use them to train its models.

Anonymous usage statistics

To understand whether the app is useful, we log one anonymous record per session containing: the session type (explore/answer), children's ages, the preset location label (e.g. "Garden"), selected materials, difficulty ratings, counts of adaptations requested, and session timing. These records contain no free text, no names, no photos, no precise location, and no device identifiers, and cannot be linked to you or your children. They are stored with Supabase (our database provider) on servers in the EU/UK region.

Children's privacy

Wonder Notes is a tool operated by parents and guardians. It is not directed at children, does not allow children to create accounts or submit personal information, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Your child's words and photos recorded in the Wonder Journal never leave your device.

Permissions the app asks for

  • Camera — only when you choose to photograph a discovery.
  • Add to Photos — only when you choose to save a record sheet or photo to your photo library.

Both are optional; the app works without them.

Your rights

Because we hold no personal data about you, there is no profile for us to access, correct, or delete — your data lives on your device under your control. If you have any questions or concerns, including under UK GDPR, contact us and we will help.

Changes

If our data practices change, we will update this page and the effective date above before the change takes effect.
